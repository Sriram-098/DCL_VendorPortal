Vendor Registration System

Important Notice: Applicants who had finally submitted the application on or after 15-JAN-2021 (after control no. 2100156) need not submit hard copies of documents.

Procurement of materials, spares & consumables by DCIL is through a system of approved/registered vendors. Vendors desirous of getting themselves as approved suppliers of DCIL may furnish complete details of the product(s) online, for which registration is desired along with credentials of the firm/establishment.

Interested applicants for registration/upgradation are requested to click on the link below for applying for Online Vendor Registration.

All the fields like General information, Registration information, Financial information etc. are to be filled with appropriate data and requested to press the "SUBMIT" button at the bottom. After successful submission of data, an Application Control Number shall be auto-generated. The auto-generated application control number and security code shall be mailed to your registered e-mail address.
