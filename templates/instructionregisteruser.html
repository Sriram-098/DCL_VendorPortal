Dredging Corporation of India Limited

Vendor Registration Instructions

Note: DCIL does not entertain middlemen or third parties. Prospective suppliers must not submit applications through them.

Instructions to Applicants:

  1. Vendors are selected based on product suitability, capacity, turnover, quality, and if needed, technical verification.
  2. Products procured based on performance require a trial process (free followed by paid).
  3. Submit/upload all notarized documents including registration, PAN, MSE, and 3 years' financials online.
  4. Processing time: 180 working days, plus 90 days for capacity assessment if applicable.
  5. DCIL reserves the right to cancel or reject any registration without assigning reasons.
  6. All forms must be signed/stamped by an authorized signatory.
  7. Vendors must respond quickly to any queries raised during evaluation.
  8. Manufacturers are given preference over dealers and traders.
  9. DCIL prefers suppliers with sound technical, financial, and ethical standards.
  10. Registration does not guarantee work or tender inclusion.
  11. Vendors must keep their data updated. For updates to name, address, or bank, reapply with documents.
  12. SC/ST and Women MSEs must submit proof of ownership and category with MSME/NSIC certificates.
  13. Startups must provide a certificate of recognition. Relaxation applies only if safety-critical items are not involved.
